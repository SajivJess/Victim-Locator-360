ABOUT VICTIM LOCATOR- 360

Introduction

Welcome to the future of disaster response! Our platform empowers the National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) with the tools to act quickly and save lives.

The system integrates rover and drone controls with real-time camera feeds, GPS tracking, and sensor data analysis. The NDRF team will be equipped with predictive machine learning insights to optimize their operations and navigate hazardous areas. Built by Code-O-Philes, it is an essential tool for effective disaster management.

Rover and Drone Controls

Seamless integration of robotic systems for search and rescue operations in disaster zones.

Live Camera Feeds

Real-time camera footage to help the team assess the situation and make better decisions.

GPS Tracking

Track the team's locations and ensure optimal coordination during the mission.

Sensor Data

Collect vital data for assessment and improve the understanding of the environment.
